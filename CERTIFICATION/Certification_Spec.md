# HHI_GOV_01 Governance Certification

## Purpose
Certify systems that meet HHI_GOV_01 governance requirements.

## Eligibility
- Named, singular stop authority
- Enforceable decision boundaries
- Mandatory escalation with logs
- Durable, auditable evidence
- Explicit reference to HHI_GOV_01

## Levels
- Certified (Score ≥ 8)
- Conditional (Score 6–7, remediation required)

## Validity
Certification valid for 12 months or until material system change.

## Use of Mark
Certified entities may display the HHI_GOV_01 Certification badge
for the validity period.
